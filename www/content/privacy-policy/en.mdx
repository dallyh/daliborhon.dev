This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use data when you visit our website, and how we use Umami, a privacy-focused analytics tool.

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by Dalibor Hon, located in Czech Republic. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.
The datacenters which are used to host this website are localed in Falkenstein, Germany and operated by [Hetzner](https://www.hetzner.com/unternehmen/rechenzentrum/).

2. What Data We Collect

We use Umami Analytics, a self-hosted, privacy-respecting analytics tool. Umami does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII).

When you visit our site, Umami collects the following non-personal data:

- Pages visited
- Referring website
- Browser type
- Device type
- Operating system
- Screen resolution
- Country (based on IP, but IP addresses are anonymized and not stored)

You can view the collected data <Link href={siteConfig.umamiShareUrl}>here</Link>.

3. How We Use This Data

The data collected is used solely for:

- Understanding how users interact with our website
- Improving content, navigation, and performance
- Monitoring website performance and technical issues

We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any invasive tracking technologies.

4. Third Parties

We do not share your data with any third parties. Our analytics data is stored on our own server, controlled solely by us.

5. Data Retention

We retain anonymized analytics data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes above. Since no personal data is collected, no user-specific data is stored or retained.

6. Your Rights (for EU/EEA Visitors)

Under the GDPR, you have rights regarding your personal data. However, since we do not collect personal data through analytics, there is no data to provide, correct, or delete.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
